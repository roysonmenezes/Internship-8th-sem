10 Real-Life AI Applications I Use Daily (with ML Explanation)

Google (Search, Maps, Gmail)
Uses machine learning to understand your query, rank results, predict routes, and filter spam based on past data and user behavior.

YouTube
ML analyzes your watch history and likes to recommend videos you are most likely to watch next.

Netflix
Uses recommendation models to suggest movies/series based on your viewing history and similar users’ preferences.

Spotify
ML studies your listening patterns to create playlists and recommend new songs you may like.

Amazon
Uses ML for product recommendations, search ranking, and predicting what you might want to buy next.

Uber
ML models predict ride prices, estimate arrival times, and match drivers with passengers efficiently.

WhatsApp
Uses ML for spam detection, message filtering, and improving features like voice-to-text and security systems.

Instagram
ML decides which posts, reels, and ads to show you based on your interactions and interests.

Paytm
Uses ML for fraud detection, spending pattern analysis, and personalized offers.

Swiggy
ML predicts delivery time, suggests restaurants, and recommends dishes based on your order history.
